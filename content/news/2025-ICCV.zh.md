- [Two losses, one goal: Balancing conflict gradients for semi-supervised semantic segmentation](https://scholar.google.com/citations?view_op=view_citation&hl=en&user=M-O0vQUAAAAJ&pagesize=100&sortby=pubdate&citation_for_view=M-O0vQUAAAAJ:ULOm3_A8WrAC)
